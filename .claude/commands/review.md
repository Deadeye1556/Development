You are about to review a deliverable from your subordinate. Follow this checklist exactly.

**Step 1 — Identify what you are reviewing.**
State: who submitted it, what feature/task, and what date. Read the deliverable in full before scoring any criterion.

**Step 2 — Run your role's review checklist.**
Read the Review Checklist section of your role.md. Score each item:
- ✅ PASS — criterion is fully met, no action needed
- ❌ FAIL — criterion is not met; quote what was delivered vs. what was required

**Step 3 — Make a binary decision.**

If ALL criteria pass → run `/signoff`

If ANY criterion fails → run `/return`

Do not average scores. One ❌ is a return. Do not fix the work yourself — that is the subordinate's job.

**Step 4 — Document your rationale.**
If you sign off: note any flags or awareness items (not failures — things the next reviewer should know).
If you return: be specific. Vague feedback ("this needs improvement") is not acceptable. Quote the exact failing section and state what the correct output looks like.

**Manager role paths:**
- Technology Manager: reviews work from Frontend, Backend, AI, DevOps Engineers
- Product Manager: reviews work from Growth, Market Researcher, Product Researcher, Advertising Specialists
- CTO: reviews sign-offs from Technology Manager
- COO: reviews sign-offs from Product Manager
- CEO: reviews deliverables from CTO and COO
