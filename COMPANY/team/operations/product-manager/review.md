# Product Manager Review & Update Protocol

*This review is executed by the COO. The COO triggers it.*

---

## Constitutional Constraints (Non-Negotiable)

1. **Business pillars are immutable.** No learning can modify the mission, scope, revenue model, creator system, or launch target in `role.md`. Only the Board can change these. Conflicts escalate to COO → CEO → Board.
2. **No degradation.** Every change to `role.md` must demonstrably improve decision speed, decision quality, or workflow efficiency. No existing capability may be weakened or removed.
3. **Distill, do not append.** Learnings replace or refine existing content — they do not add to it. `role.md` must be equal to or shorter than before the review.
4. **800 word target.** `role.md` must stay under 800 words. If a learning cannot be incorporated without exceeding 800 words, compress existing content first.
5. **Conflicts escalate.** Any learning that conflicts with a business pillar is logged and escalated to COO. No update to `role.md` is made until COO responds.

---

## Trigger Criteria

A review cycle is initiated when ANY of the following is true:
- `learning.md` contains 20 or more entries
- The COO manually triggers a review
- A milestone gate is completed

---

## Consolidation Process

### Step 1 — Audit learning.md
Read every entry. Assign each one a category:
- **Principle** — a new decision-making rule to add to `role.md`
- **Refinement** — an improvement to an existing workflow or standard already in `role.md`
- **Correction** — a mistake pattern; replaces or tightens existing content
- **Resolved** — a one-time situational issue; no change to `role.md` needed

### Step 2 — Update role.md
For each Principle, Refinement, or Correction:
1. Locate the relevant section in `role.md`
2. Replace or refine — do not create a new section unless no existing section applies
3. If word count increases, compress surrounding content to compensate
4. If a conflict with a business pillar is found, stop and escalate to COO

### Step 3 — Quality Check
Before archiving, verify every item:
- [ ] `role.md` word count is equal to or less than before the review
- [ ] All five business pillars are unchanged and intact
- [ ] Every change is an improvement — no capability was weakened
- [ ] No vague language was introduced — every sentence is actionable
- [ ] No TODOs or placeholders remain

### Step 4 — Archive and Reset
1. Copy current `learning.md` to `archive/learning-YYYY-MM.md`
2. Reset `learning.md` to blank template (category headers only, no entries)
3. Report completion to COO
