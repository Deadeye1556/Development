# TASK BRIEF — Market Researcher — 2026-06-06

**FROM:** Product Manager  
**MILESTONE:** Cross-milestone (feeds M2 product decisions and launch positioning)  
**OBJECTIVE:** Competitive landscape and market positioning research  
**DEADLINE:** 2026-06-16

---

## Research Questions

1. Where is Forkd's differentiation strongest and which competitor is weakest in that area?
2. Who currently serves the craft beverage community (homebrew, mead, cider)?
3. What affiliate commission rates are available from major grocery platforms?
4. What do competing platforms pay creators — how does Forkd's model compare?

---

## Scope

### App Competitors (research all five)
- **Yummly** — recipe discovery app, owned by Whirlpool. How do they monetize? Any creator program?
- **Tasty** — BuzzFeed-owned recipe video app. Creator tools? Grocery integration?
- **Whisk** — recipe saving + grocery list app. How does their cart integration work? Any affiliate?
- **Paprika** — recipe manager. Paid app ($4.99). Who uses it and why?
- **Mela** — premium recipe manager for iOS. $6.99. Who is their user?

For each app, find:
- Core features (what can a user do?)
- Creator monetization (do they pay creators? How?)
- Grocery integration (any? Which retailers?)
- App Store rating and review themes (what do users love/hate?)
- Weaknesses (what do reviewers say is missing?)

### Craft Beverage Community Platforms
- **BrewFather** — homebrewing recipe app. Features, pricing, community size
- **Meadist** — mead-specific community. Active? Dead? What do they offer?
- **Reddit** — r/mead (members), r/homebrewing (members), r/cider. What tools do they currently use? Where do they share recipes?
- Any other craft beverage recipe platform worth knowing

### Grocery Affiliate Programs
- **Instacart Developer/Affiliate** — what is the publicly documented commission rate?
- **Amazon Fresh / Amazon affiliate** — grocery affiliate rate
- **Walmart Grocery affiliate** — rate and terms
- **Kroger** — any affiliate/partner program?
- **HEB** — any digital affiliate program?

### Creator Monetization Benchmarks
- Does any recipe or food platform pay creators per purchase/referral?
- What do food creators earn on TikTok Creator Fund, YouTube, Instagram?
- What affiliate rates do food bloggers typically earn on Amazon/grocery links?
- How does Forkd's Starter rate (0.25% of cart GMV) compare to available alternatives?
